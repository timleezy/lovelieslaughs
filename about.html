ABOUT

Briding The Communication Gap Between The Sexes

Madame Me
Our show is notorious for shining the spotlight on those hard to discuss relationship / life issues. Love Lies & Laughs fosters communication and growth between couples and aids in quality identifiers for singles.
We have an unconventional method which delivers these issues in a hilariously sexy yet thought evoking way. We provide a judgement free zone and encourage our guests to share realistic experiences with our listeners.

Lamika Jenkins—Ellis

Love Lies & Laughs is the brain child of Lamika Jenkins-Ellis which she debuted in May of 2011. Lamika figured if you put a mature audience in a sexy, chic, & judgement free atmosphere people would start to open up and give their honest opinions on various risqué/racy issues that plague most relationships... During these processes she found that people just simply want to be heard, they want to share their experiences and absorb the experiences of others. Believe it or not hearing the same information from a third party is more notable than hearing it from your partner... She also found that her innovative out of the box thinking would serve as an unconventional vehicle to aid in bridging the gap of communication between men & women. In a sense Lamika was formulating her version of a social experiment. Due to the overwhelming positive response she was able to take her interactive adult discussion segments to broader platforms hence... LLL Radio Show, LLL Stage Play, & LLL Live Soirée which will be coming to a city near you... “Therapy Without The Couch”
