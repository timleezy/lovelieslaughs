Relationship Coaching

Grow With Love Lies & Laughs

Love Lies & Laughs Relationship Coaching fosters communication, growth & the art of sensual connection. We also aid in quality identifiers, self reflection, & exploring new ideas and perspectives all while offering an empathetic ear and uplifting words of support. We encourage our clients to get “Naked in The Truth” which is the purest form of vulnerability...

Life throws many surprises at us, and while we cannot predict the outcome of any situation, we can take control of how we choose to respond to the situation. With our coaching sessions, you’ll learn about acceptance and how to exert your power of choice over whatever life may throw at you.

We have assisted singles & couples of various orientations and lifestyles since 2007 with improving their relationship success by providing them with a non-clinical, friendly, safe environment to open up about those hard to discuss relationship/life issues such as dating concerns, intimacy frustrations, insecurities, secret love lives, social expectations, reentering the dating world, and much more...

  • Singles One on One Sessions (60 or 120min)
  • Singles Seminars (max 20 attendees) (90min)
  • Couples One on One Sessions (60 or 120min)
  • Couples Seminars (max 10 couples) (90min)
  • Telephone Session (30 or 60min)
  • Video Session (30 or 60min)
